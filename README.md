# c3-UNLOCKED

Android app for browsing media.ccc.de, streaming talks, downloading videos in the desired language and watching saved talks later from the app.

## Current Features

- Browse all talks and conference/event collections from the public media.ccc.de API.
- Search events and open talk detail screens with metadata, tags, and links.
- Pick preferred MP4 recordings and send downloads to Android public Downloads under `Download/c3-UNLOCKED/...`.
- Track queued/running/completed download state in-app.
- Watch downloaded files or remote recordings with resume-position persistence.

## A Note of Gratitude

The CC architecture has been an endless source of inspiration, knowledge, and joy. Thank you for documenting, translating, and sharing decades of talks, workshops, and chaos with the world. This is my small way of giving back.

## How This Came to Be

I built this app from scratch because I wanted a mobile-friendly way to enjoy CCC content. After finishing my first version, I discovered [ChaosFlix](https://github.com/NiciDieNase/chaosflix) — an older app with a similar idea. [ChaosFlix](https://github.com/NiciDieNase/chaosflix) served its purpose well, but it's showing its age. Rather than letting my work sit unused, I decided to publish this as a modern alternative for today's mobile devices.
